Mankav Kalam

'Mankav Kalam' is a century-old Kalam (house of the agrarian family) with an 80-year-old Pathayapura(granary). Located in the modest village of Pallasena, this homestay peacefully resides in the backdrop of Nelliyampathy hills surrounded by lush green paddy fields.

About Mankav Kalam

Think of a stay at Mankav Kalam as an ideal place to escape the mundane activities of your daily life. It is located on 10 acres of land and caters to three spacious rooms that have been personally restored by its owners that come with a story. You shall be attended by two members of the house staff and spoil you with their hospitality.

Mankav kalam was previously the family home of Unniytans. Since the couple grew old and their 2 children away from the native, they decided to sell the property. Their nephew brought out the plan to a group of 5 friends, who were interested in investing in their childhood village and give a new meaning to the place and bought Mankav Kalam.

Directly operated by Moksha Stories, Mankav Kalam provides our guests with an unmatchable staying experience that is loyal to the culture and ethos of Palakkad. It also promises scenic morning trails to forever fields and faraway hills.

Mankav Kalam can host up to nine people with the support of extra beds. It has two double rooms which are air-conditioned while the third one is a single room. This heritage home also has a common space, a treehouse, and a pond.

Even though we provide wifi for our guests, we discourage them from using them. We also have a well-appointed kitchen.

Our homestay takes pride in having one of the most beautiful verandahs that provide our guests a 180-degree view of the farmlands and cloud-kissed mountains.

At Mankav Kalam, we only serve homely Keralean food indigenous to Palakkad. So, be ready to taste lots of coconut in many of the dishes we serve here as the cuisine demands its generous application. Also, we serve the famous Palakkadan Matta (brown rice with higher contents of bran), which we harvest on our paddy fields.

Krishna Vilas

Located in the beautiful village of Ramaserry, Palakkad; Krishna Vilas is an 80-year-old elegant tharavad, a best-kept family secret. Influenced by Tamil architecture, this heritage home is a place to detox and de-tech.

About Krishna Vilas

Krishna Vilas was built in 1930 by Shri M Krishnan. This heritage property was built as a gift for his daughter Subhadra for bringing luck and prosperity to the family. It is a tharavad; which follows a matrilineal system of the joint family by the Nairs in Kerala.

Spending a considerable amount of time in Tamil Nadu, Shri M Krishnan Nair built Krishna Vilas having influenced by Tamil architecture. This property features a courtyard that has two wings; centered between the main building and kitchen, a voluminous dining hall, terrace, a stylish veranda, and a garden rightly fit to relax.

Having stood the test of time, Krishna Vilas was revived and restored by the great- grandson of Shri M Krishnan Nair and his wife. The details of carpentry in this heritage property remain untouched without making any sweeping changes, a testimony to the great artisanship of the time.

Facilities

Dining

Getting here

Krishna Vilas can host up to 9 people. This heritage home has 3 double rooms and 1 suite. The rooms are large with high ceilings, gleaming floors, and polished windows and doors that characterize the comfort and luxury of a very warm and intimate kind. This property has a beautiful interactive kitchen and its staff members are rooted in the culture and tradition of Palakkad, Kerala.

At Krishna Vilas, our staff members would be happy to serve you some delicious Indian dishes of your choice. But, while being at Ramaserry, we suggest that you do not miss out on tasting the famous Ramaserry Idlis which is very unique.

The nearest airport is Coimbatore which is 1 hr30 min away (Kochi -2 hrs 45 mins). Olavakkode Junction is the nearest train station to Krishna Vilas and takes 25minutes to commute. Upon request, we can organize transfers.

Srivilas

Situated in the modest village of Mangode, Palakkad, Srivilas is a 350 year old traditional home with an elegant architecture. An ideal escape for people seeking to slow down a busy life.

About Srivilas

Srivilas is a 350 year old traditional home located in 4 acres of land. Its current Mr. Appuraj has given utmost important in retaining its magnificence. Our guests are guaranteed to feel uniqueness in its features. Srivilas creates an aura of healing for all who enter here.

The architecture of Srivilas is in sound with the terrain and provides personal spaces in abundance with the nature. The closeness to raw nature forms a central focus of this heritage home and forms a perfect cocoon to a holiday.

Srivilas is located between the modest towns of Koduvayur and Pudunagram, Palakkad. One can also take a ride to visit Pollachi which is only one hour ride from this town. For any cultural escapades or community visits, we can have it arranged for you.

Aalpura Heritage

A century-old traditional farmhouse, Aalapura Heritage is located in the granary of Palakkad, Chittur. Equipped with an Agri museum, a pool, and cocoa plantations, this heritage home makes it an idyllic place for people seeking to escape into nature.

About Aalapura Heritage

Built by the great grandfather of the present owner Mr. Gopinath Mannadiar, Aalpura Heritage is a 150-year-old farmhouse. This place is a perfect reminisce of a period of life in its simplicity.

Surrounded by a tropical garden containing coconut, cocoa, different medicinal herbs, a pond, a livestock farm, and green paddy fields, Aalapura heritage transmits a vibe that integrates our mind, body, and soul. This property also has a well-maintained swimming pool which makes an effective recreation for all.

The in-house agro museum adds charm to this heritage property. This museum has beautifully displayed artifacts that represent elements of rural life, including transport, crafts, farming activities, and its dwelling.

Accommodation at Aalpura Heritage consists of three cottages. It can comfortably occupy 9 people and makes a perfect place for a family get-together. All the view from the cottages offers a spectacular view of nature and its splendor. The cottages and the main building host nice balconies and verandahs where you can indulge in some nice conversation overlooking the garden.

Meenikode Kalam

A splendid heritage home, Meenikode Kalam is located in the modest village of Vadavannur, Palakkad. Built more than a century ago, this homestay is a perfect place to enjoy solitude, and witness an ambiance that transports you to the past.

About Meenikode Kalam

This property and the surrounding land were received as a gift for Late Ms. Meenakshi Amma from her husband. In the year 1854, they constructed a magnificent home and named it Meenikode Kalam. She was then honored as the great matriarch of the family.

Meenikode Kalam is a grandeur of legacy. This heritage home was restored carefully, by its fifth generation without compromising the character of this place. In the courtyard of this property, a very large bronze vessel calledUrurli has been placed. It is a cooking vessel of the past and signifies the prosperity of the family.

This heritage home is also located near the birth house of the legendary actor M.G Ramachandran, a.k.a MGR.
