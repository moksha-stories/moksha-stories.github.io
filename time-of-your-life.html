Couple goals / Honeymoon

Discover each other by traversing thru the narrowd pathways of little hamlets. Begin your marriage with stories, adventure, and of course sumptuous food.

Together with Friends

Old friends don't just bring laughter they bring a ton of memories too. Relish freshly brewed toddy, sing, and dance to the folk songs at the end of the day after immersing yourself in the joy of our exciting tours.

Family time

Happiness is only real when shared they say, but it is not just real it feels cozy and peaceful when shared with family. Enjoy your travel with family and leave the rest to us after all we see each of our customers as an extended adopted family.

Fly like a free bird

There is something about solitude and travel. One makes you discover yourself and the other makes you discover new land. We understand both and provide the required impetus for you to travel without a mate but with the guidance of our tailor- made tours and tranquil homestays.

Stay and explore with the community

While traveling alone can be fun it becomes even more exciting when you make new friends during a solo trip. Explore new hillocks and streams with a stranger from another state or even a different country. We ensure you by the end of it these strangers develop a strange bond. A bond of community, a bond of being a nomad. We call it the nomadic bond. A bond that might just remain intact all your life.

Follow your passion

Have a passion to write, create music or maybe just meditate in seclusion? Our stays are a perfect place to clear a writer's block or maybe find your mojo in music. Enable your lost passion and follow your heart we absolutely don't mind being meager spectators to your dreams.

Learn Something new

Pottery, folk dance, folk songs, instrument making there is a lot to learn from our beloved artists who are always ready to give you lessons. We will ensure that you at least learn the basics, enough for you to show off to your peers and family. Being testimonial for the fun you had.

Research

Study the old art forms or go further and research old houses and their history. We have your backs for anything that builds more stories.

Announced event

We cater to various events for different purposes. It could be a walking tour, an art expo, a musical evening, or even adventurous trekking. You tell us we will mobilize the resources for it.

Corporate events

An offsite amidst the lush green paddy fields followed by a team-building activity, our corporate events have a flavour of their own and will not only rejuvenate your employees but will also help them carry new memories and build lasting team bonding with all the luxury you need to meet your corporate policy.

Celebrations

Destination Weddings, Engagement, Mehendi, Sangeet, and any social gathering can be catered by us in some great destinations. Be assured we will never make it look mundane or just another celebration

The golden years

The golden years should not stop you from exploring. We welcome the folks living the golden years of their lives to explore and travel with us without any qualms or worries. Our stays, tours, and travel pundits will help you actually live your golden years in complete freedom from all the burdens of routine

Engaging people with determination

Traveling is not just about Instagram posts or vlogging. It is a divine experience that needs to move beyond the realm of just one sense. It is the scent of mud at a potter’s home, the sound of musical instruments in a folk song, the beats of the anklets in folk dance, and the aroma of freshly brewed filter coffee in am agraharam. All our tours can be made absolutely easy for access to differently-abled.
